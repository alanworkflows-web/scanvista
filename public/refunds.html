ScanVista Cancellation & Refund Policy

Cancellations

Users can cancel their flat plan at any time right from their dashboard. Upon cancellation, your access to editing sheets will continue running smoothly until the current billing cycle expires.

Refunds

Due to the instant delivery of QR printing access and our digital infrastructure, all payments are non-refundable. If you cancel your subscription, you will not receive a refund for the current billing cycle.
